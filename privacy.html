Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Short version:
Picksy does not collect, store, or share personal data. The app works completely offline.

1. Overview

Picksy is a lightweight random generator app designed for fun, quick decisions, and group games. We respect your privacy and built the app with a strict privacy-first approach.

2. Data Collection

Picksy does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

  • No account system
  • No analytics tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No cookies

3. Local Data Storage

All generated content (history, favorites, custom lists, settings, language preferences) is stored locally on your device only using secure on-device storage. This data never leaves your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

4. In-App Purchases

Optional Pro features are offered via in-app purchases. Payments are processed securely by:

  • Google Play Store
  • Apple App Store

We do not receive, store, or process payment information. Payment handling is governed by the respective store's privacy policy.

5. Third-Party Services

Picksy does not integrate analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies. The only third-party services involved are the official app distribution platforms for payment processing.

6. External Links

The app may contain optional links to external websites such as GitHub or Buy Me a Coffee. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites.

7. Children's Privacy

Picksy does not knowingly collect personal data from children. Since no personal data is collected at all, the app is safe to use for general audiences.

8. Your Rights (EEA / GDPR)

As Picksy does not collect or process personal data, there is no personal information stored that can be accessed, corrected, or deleted.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
